Our Journey

2021 - Concept Development

Developed the AI algorithms that would power our business model. Focused on product sourcing and pricing optimization.

2022 - System Testing

Ran beta tests with a small product catalog. Optimized AI for the South African market and Western Cape logistics.

2023 - Official Launch

Launched with 15 rare supplements. AI system managed all aspects from day one.

2024 - Expansion

Expanded product catalog to 50+ supplements. Started serving all of South Africa with AI-optimized logistics.

How Our AI System Works

Product Sourcing

Our AI scans global suppliers 24/7 to find rare supplements at the best prices. It analyzes quality, shipping times, and reliability.

Content Creation

AI generates compelling product descriptions, creates marketing content, and designs product images tailored to our audience.

Order Fulfillment

Automated system processes orders, coordinates with suppliers, and manages shipping through Courier Guy and Paxi Lockers.

Customer Engagement

AI chatbots handle inquiries, send shipping notifications, and provide personalized recommendations.

Marketing Automation

AI creates and schedules social media content across all platforms, optimized for engagement and conversions.

Tax & Compliance

Automatically calculates VAT, generates tax reports, and ensures SARS compliance without human intervention.
